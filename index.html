My Hobby: Sports Analytics

My friends and I are passionate sports fans who gather together to watch sports events regularly. Inevitably, we get into arguements about which team is better and why. Eventually, we decided to use stats and measurable metrics to argue with each other sparking a passion of ours, sports analytics. We began to gather our data and read through stat sheets to have a better understand of the game. Eventually, we made a sports analytics club at school where we can open the debate of "greatest of all time" or "who's going to win" to everyone else.
